
1. 6 decks of cards. No hole card for dealer.

2. Minimum bet: 300. Maximum bet: 10,000. Must be 100's multiple.

3. Dealers stand on soft 17 (Ex: A & 6).

4. Surrender returns 50% of placed bets. But, not allowed against dealer's Ace.

5. Blackjack (a combo of A & 10-valued card) pays 3 to 2, which is a profit 150% of placed bets.

6. A combo of Ace & 10-valued card after split is ordinary 21, not Blackjack. Blackjack restricts to initial 2 cards of a hand only.

7. If dealer's 1st card is Ace, players can choose to buy insurance by half of original bets. Insurance pays 2 to 1 if dealer has Blackjack. Otherwise, players lose insurance bets.

8. Identically-valued combos (like J & 10) can split 3 times to 4 hands at most, except for Aces, with just once per hand.

9. No hits allowed for split Aces.

10. Double down on any 2 cards is allowed, except for Blackjack and split Aces. Double down automatically draws exactly 1 more card to make your hand with 3 cards.
